Project Title: Stock Turnover Ratio Analysis

Objectives:
1. Perform detailed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on stock turnover ratios and related variables.
2. Visualize trends and seasonality in turnover ratios over time.
3. Compute rolling statistics (mean, volatility) to identify regime shifts.
4. Identify periods of unusually high or low trading activity and provide business interpretations.
5. Prepare reproducible scripts and visual outputs for presentation.

Learning Outcomes:
- Real-world financial data preprocessing (date parsing, missing data handling).
- Time series visualization and statistical analysis using Python (pandas, matplotlib).
- Identification of trends, seasonality, and volatility patterns in turnover ratios.
- Translating statistical findings into actionable financial insights and investment implications.

Business Insights (examples):
- Persistent increases in turnover ratio could indicate rising liquidity and investor interest; sudden spikes may signal news-driven trading or rebalancing.
- Low turnover over prolonged periods can indicate illiquid markets and higher transaction costs for large trades.
- Comparing rolling volatility and mean can highlight changing market regimes useful for portfolio allocation decisions.
